# Style

## Setting

Set in 1346 in a fictional area in north England.

The Priory of Our Lady of Thorns is a small priory (not a full
monastery). It probably used to have 20 full monks and 20 lay brothers,
but plagues, crop failures, and wars have led to its decline. A certain
sense of two-centuries-old and dusty and worn is apparent in descriptions.

That doesn't mean all of the characters are old or dusty.

## Writing

- not jokey, except when player is trying to do cliche IF things,
  like XYZZY

- British English, not American, and avoiding modern phrasing.
  The game should sound like how Aldwin might speak (except not
  Middle English, obviously)

- Latin phrasings on things the monks write. Often translate for the player
  except in cases where it is probably easy to guess meaning

- Aldwin ALWAYS refers to brothers as "Brother Foo" out of respect.
  Other monks might refer to him more casually, or to each other.

- Accentuate weather, temp, light, smell, sound.

- Many things can be smelled (Aldwin is studying herbology!)

- Remigio is uneducated and a refugee from Tuscany. His speech is
  broken English-Italian. It's not meant to be mean, though, so make
  sure it doesn't sound Mario-cliche or such.

- Aelred's death is a big emotional center to the story and Aldwin's
  reactions to it, so it should come up in related place
  (player wears his old gloves, etc)

- Wilfred is the other main emotional character. He should always be
  kindly. He should be the obvious person to confess to at the point
  Aldwin might like to. In the worst ending (inaction), Wilfred dies.


## IF Stuff

Aldwin has been living here for months, so he knows the space well and
has even been in the Crypt once. He hasn't been in the Crypt Vestry.
He doesn't know about the FitzAlan Crypt (and perhaps the younger monks
also don't know, since its unused for a well)
