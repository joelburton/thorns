## Special Actions

These are special actions; they don't use a turn to perform and operate outside the game world:

- `EXITS`: show exits from location
- `HELP`: see short help
- `HERE`: shows monks in the location
- `HINTS`: context-aware hint menus
- `MAP`: show map of the area
- `OBJECTS`: lists objects you've handled
- `PLACES`: list places you've been
- `QUOTES ON`/`OFF`: turn on/off box quotes
- `RESTORE`: restore saved game state
- `SAVE`: save game state
- `UNDO`: undo most recent turn
- `WHO`: lists all monks in game

Time and the passing of is important. You can use:

- `TIME`: gives estimate of current time
- `WAIT` *n* `HOURS`/`MINUTES`: wait this amount of time
- `WAIT UNTIL` *hh:mm*: wait until given time (12h time)

There are other commands that will be revealed during game play.
